Almond Jell-O with Lychee

Discription

Light and refreshing! Also very pretty.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 (.25 ounce) packages unflavored gelatin powder
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 cups milk
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons almond extract
  • 8 lychees, or to taste

Steps to make the dish

Step 1

IPour 1 cup water into a bowl; stir in gelatin powder until dissolved.
Slowly stir boiling water into gelatin mixture.
Mix milk, sugar, and almond extract into gelatin mixture; add lychees.
Pour gelatin mixture into a shallow container; refrigerate until set, 3 to 4 hours.
